Our Story

How It All Began

From a family kitchen in Juárez to the mountains of New Mexico

La Finca de Villa was born from a simple belief: that the best food comes from family, tradition, and love. Owner Ruben Moreno grew up in Juárez, Chihuahua — where his parents, originally from Puebla, Mexico, raised him surrounded by the rich flavors of authentic Mexican cooking.

Ruben learned to cook from his mother, who ran her own restaurant in Mexico. Those recipes, passed down through generations, became the foundation of everything we serve today. When Ruben moved to the United States in 2008 and settled in Angel Fire, New Mexico, he brought those flavors with him.

With the support of the Angel Fire community, Ruben opened his first restaurant in 2022 under the name Mi Cocina Mexicana. It quickly became a local favorite — a place where mountain visitors and locals alike could enjoy authentic, homemade Mexican food that tasted like it came from someone's grandmother's kitchen.

The Moreno Family

Growing together, one location at a time

In January 2026, Ruben rebranded the restaurant as La Finca de Villa — "The Ranch of Villa" — a name that reflects the rustic, welcoming spirit of the restaurant and its mountain surroundings.

His daughter Itzel, an accounting graduate from the University of New Mexico, joined her father to help manage operations. Together, they expanded to a second location in Red River on Christmas Eve, December 24, 2025 — opening inside the Lifts West Condominium Resort Hotel on West Main Street.

The Moreno family's story is one of hard work, heritage, and heart. La Finca de Villa isn't just a restaurant — it's a dream built by a family, for the community.

Red River, NM

The mountain keeps growing

The opening of La Finca de Villa's Red River location was a milestone moment for the family. Located inside Lifts West Condominium Resort Hotel on West Main Street, the second location brings the same authentic homemade flavors to one of northern New Mexico's most beloved mountain towns.

Red River is known for its ski slopes, summer festivals, and small-town charm. La Finca de Villa is proud to be part of that story — open year-round, seven days a week, serving the mountain community and visitors alike.
