Family Owned & Operated Since 2022

A family recipe for every mountain moment

La Finca de Villa brings the warmth of a Mexican family kitchen to the mountains of northern New Mexico. Owner Ruben Moreno grew up in Juárez, Chihuahua, learning authentic recipes from his mother. Every dish reflects the culinary heritage of both Northern and Southern Mexico — fresh, homemade, and made with heart.

From Our Kitchen

House Favorites

Authentic, homemade dishes crafted with fresh ingredients and traditional flavors.

Beef Fajitas

Charbroiled beef on a sizzling cast iron skillet with bell peppers, onions, rice, beans, guacamole & pico de gallo.

Shrimp Fajitas

Jumbo shrimp on a sizzling skillet with grilled veggies, melted white cheese, rice, beans & fresh guacamole.

Chile Rellenos

Two hand-battered poblano peppers, lightly fried to perfection, served with rice, beans & fresh pico salad.

Margaritas

Classic on the rocks or frozen and fruity — handcrafted margaritas to complement every meal.
