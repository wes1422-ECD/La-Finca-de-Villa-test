## About the Restaurant

### Overview
La Finca de Villa (formerly Mi Cocina Mexicana) is a family-owned Mexican restaurant serving authentic, homemade dishes crafted with fresh ingredients and traditional flavors rooted in both Northern and Southern Mexican culinary traditions.

### Owner & Family
- **Proprietor**: Ruben Moreno
- **Manager**: Itzel Moreno (Ruben's daughter; accounting degree, University of New Mexico)
- **Background**: Ruben's parents are originally from Puebla, Mexico and later relocated to Juárez, Chihuahua, where Ruben grew up. He learned to cook from his mother, who ran her own restaurant in Mexico.
- Ruben moved to the U.S. in 2008 and worked for a real estate developer in Angel Fire. With that community support he opened Mi Cocina Mexicana in Angel Fire in 2022.
- Itzel joined to help manage operations, and together they expanded to Red River, opening the second location on December 24, 2025 inside Lifts West.

### Story / About Copy (Draft — based on Taos News article + known facts)
> La Finca de Villa brings the warmth of a Mexican family kitchen to the mountains of northern New Mexico. Owner Ruben Moreno grew up in Juárez, Chihuahua, learning authentic recipes from his mother — who ran her own restaurant in Mexico. He brought those flavors north when he opened his first location in Angel Fire in 2022, and in 2025 expanded to Red River. Every dish reflects the culinary heritage of both Northern and Southern Mexico: fresh, homemade, and made with heart.

## Brand & Design Notes

### Logo
- Wooden barn/ranch gate design with arched top
- Text: "LA FINCA" (arch top), "DE VILLA" (center, bold), "Mexican Restaurant" (subtitle below gate)
- Decorative elements: Red chili ristras (hanging peppers) on each side
- Central icon: Green bowl with flame/chili icon (red, yellow, green — representing fresh, hot food)
- Background: Warm parchment/cream texture
- Style: Rustic hacienda, ranch aesthetic — warm, authentic, inviting

### Brand Colors (extracted from logo)
- **Primary**: Warm brown / wood tones (#6B3A1F range)

- **Accent Red**: Chili red (#C0392B range)
- **Accent Green**: Fresh green (#27AE60 range)
